About Us

BAI Security is a nationally recognized security auditing and compliance firm that specializes in providing clients in highly regulated sectors with the most innovative, comprehensive, and affordable security solutions in our industry. Among numerous accolades, we’ve most recently been honored as the Best Security Solution Providers of 2020.

We endeavor to ensure that organizations of all sizes have access to truly top-shelf audit and compliance services in the most cost-effective manner possible.

Our Services

As a pure play IT security provider, BAI Security delivers uncommon specialization compared to most providers. Unlike general IT or consulting companies with outsourced staff, we at BAI live and breathe IT security, with assessment and compliance as our singular priority.

Our dedicated team of entirely in-house engineers and seasoned auditors devote significant resources to researching present-day hacking tools and cybercriminals’ most aggressive techniques. We then reverse-engineer our methods, developing assessment processes that mimic the expertise, patience, and tenacity found in modern-day hackers.

BAI’s approach to assessment and compliance is intentionally exhaustive. We stand in contrast to traditional, ‘bare minimum’ approaches that satisfy regulators and well-intended IT departments, but which are not comprehensive enough to stop modern-day hackers. To keep your organization secure, our robust methods are far more comprehensive in breadth and depth compared to traditional assessments.

And yet our forward thinking team never get too comfortable. With cybercriminals rapidly changing their attack fronts, BAI engineers continually re-test methods, challenge assumptions, and iterate proactive solutions to stay ahead of emerging threats. This positions us at the frontline of cybersecurity, undermining even the savviest hackers and raising the bar for the entire security industry.

With thousands of clients across highly regulated sectors, BAI Security has built a reputation as a trusted and ongoing security ally with an unmatched commitment to service. In fact, when asked about their overall BAI experience, 92% of BAI clients rate their overall BAI experience “Very Good” or “Excellent.”

Our team is always ready to help our clients with any challenges they might have – before, during, and well beyond engagements. Our clients know they can call us any time to speak with people they already know and who are familiar with their environment. We also don’t outsource anything – which means you know you’re protected exclusively by our team of in-house experts.

By emphasizing open communication and providing helpful, flexible service that goes far beyond the audit, we pride ourselves in the successful long-term relationships we develop with our clients.

As specialists, we know how to zero in on your organization’s unique needs. We will custom-build your security package with exactly what you want. And, harkening back to our Mission to serve organizations of all sizes with top-shelf services, our offerings sit at a price point that allow clients with varied budgets to enjoy the highest quality security services available.
