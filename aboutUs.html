My Process

Step 1

Communicate the goals of the website. Who am I building for and why?

Step 2

User & Competitor Research. Investigate who the users would be and what my competitors have already done. Create a persona to identify the users.

Step 3

Explore how I can solve business and user goals. Create wireframes and test concepts.

Step 4

Refine the concept and create a detailed design for the best solution. All about prototypes.

Step 5

Create the solution. This is all about coding the website according to the design plan.

Step 6

How did the solution measure up to the goals set at the beginning? Get feedback and repeat the steps until the product is satisfactory.
