BAI Security

Details

Client: BAI Security (via 99designs.com)

Tech: HTML5, CSS3, SASS, Bootstrap, Fontawesome, Google Fonts, Adobe Photoshop

View Website

Description

Overview

Bai Security is a cybersecurity company offering their services to businesses who want to protect their networks. After years of business, they decided to redesign their website to meet the standards of the modern world. This website would be professional, modern, and visually identify their status as a technology company. I designed a website with the Bootstrap framework for Bai Security.

The images were obtained from pixabay.com.

Challenge

The main challenge was to modernize every aspect of Bai Security's website while retaining all of their current information.

Process

01

The project began as a simple wireframe to organize the content. After gathering the assets from BAI Security's site, the wireframe steadily developed into a mockup. The challenge was to keep the same content on the page, but organize it into an easier to read format. Adding visual interest with pictures and separating the text into bundles of related information make it easier on the eyes of the reader.

02

Bai Security requested a site that is professional, modern, and displays their identity as a tech company. With that in mind, the hero images were chosen to complement the idea of security and modern technology. It is important for a brand to keep their logo and color theme. I determined the color scheme for the website based on their theme and chose images with similar color schemes. The audience for the website are other businesses. A professional font set is always appreciated.

03

In coding the website, the buttons on the homepage with shortcuts to important information were made to stand out with color, size, and styling.

04

The about page was full of text and coded to condense some information in an accordion, others made visually appealing with images, icons, and a hierarchy to entice the reader.

05

The contact page is made simple with contact information and an email form for convenience.
